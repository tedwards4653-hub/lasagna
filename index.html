Lasagna, also known by its plural form lasagne, is a type of pasta made in wide, flat sheets. It's known best for its use in baked dishes made by stacking layers of pasta with filling. Fillings can alternate, with fillings such as ragu (ground meats and tomato sauce), bechamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.
