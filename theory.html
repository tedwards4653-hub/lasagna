Secret Lasagna Theory

The secret lasagna theory is a long-standing pasta ideology well-known in the world of ardent pasta lovers. At its core, the Lasagna Theory suggests the existence of a supernatural entity that was the cause of the origin of the recipe for pasta in the minds of humanity. The theory reasons that had lasagna not been created, humanity would be less dependent on the lasagna-makers, who have become infamous in the centuries since lasagna's creation for their long-winded pyramid scheme named the "P.A.S.T.A.M.A.K.E.R.S.", or alternatively, the "Noodley Pasta Saucey Eggy Oniony Tomato Pastey Mozzarella Cheesy Ground Beef And Sausagey Layer And Bake Combine Cheese Mixture In An Oven" for short. This supernatural being is thought to be waiting on humanity's downfall, and most likely in kahoots with the plants. See also the current scientific knowledge on plants farming us. Throughout human history, the Lasagna Theory's influece has changed profoundly. In its early days, fervent supporters of the Lasagna Theory, known as the "cheesy ones", were widespread and highly regarded in society. However, as time has passed, the group has declined in size and influence, especially in the the last few presidential administrations. Notably, the chances of a cheesy president being elected to office have declined dramatically in the past 40 years. This interesting trend can most likely be attributed to the shocking event of the Lasagna Massacre of '24, in which groups of people pelted each other with lasagna in the streets of Versailles, due to suspicion of an "impasta" among them, a non-cheesy one. (The term "massacre", of course, is a result of the sheer amount of lasagna that pasta way.) After all of this, the Lasagna Theory has become a signifcantly more fringe belief, and knowledge of its history has been lost to time over generations.
