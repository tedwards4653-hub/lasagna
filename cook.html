How to make Lasagna

Some nice music for while you make the pasta ^^^^

Online Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef & sausage
  • Cheeses
  • Apple sauce
  • Pie dough
  • Oatmeal
  • Ice cream
  • Egg
  • Any additional spices!

Items needed:

  • Oven
  • Large pot
  • 9x13 dish
  • House (preferably yours)
  • Working plumbing system
  • Sink
  • Emergency fund (in case something goes wrong)
  • Browser-capable device with display (to read the instructions)
  • Bowl

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Mix in oatmeal and pie dough
  4. Combine cheese mixture with apple sauce
  5. Layer with a swirl of ice cream & bake
